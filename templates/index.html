How to Use

Download your resume from resume.io as a PDF file.

Open the application, enter your resume renderingToken and click the download button. It will automatically download your resume as a PDF.

How to find your renderingToken

Resumes: https://resume.io/api/app/resumes

Cover Letters: https://resume.io/api/app/cover-letters/

You will see a list of your resumes. Find the resume you want to download and get the renderingToken.
